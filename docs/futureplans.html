Future Plans

As I continue to spiral, my future plans get more and more absurd every day. I thought it would be silly to keep a log of my ideas.

But the thing is they give you a stipend, but not a living salary. So I started looking into remote jobs, and I applied to a few like electrical engineering 3-line diagrams type stuff. So to sum this up I would get a full time job that I am doing remotely- which is really not a thing for electrical engineers- then move to Taiwan whenever the research internship starts. When I'm in Taiwan, I apply to master's programs, which would start in Fall 2027. So this puts me at spending at least 2 years in Taiwan, a place that I did not think about until last week. Oh, I also need to finish my undergrad at some point. That could be in the Fall at Stevens, or through random transfer credits, depending when I ultimately get asked to move to Taiwan. This is truly the most insane plan that has ever been devised. I would need so many things to go my way. I would at least like to apply to schools and research in Taiwan because I feel like I never actually put effort into applying to international programs before and it would be exciting if it worked out. But, if all else fails, which it most likely will, I will just stay enrolled at Stevens. This is entirely separate from what I'm going to do this summer, which is actually a more pressing issue.
